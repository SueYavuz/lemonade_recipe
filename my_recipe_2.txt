Lemonade Recipe:
Recipe:
Dissolve sugar in water in a blender until completely dissolved.
Squees all three lemons on top of your sweet water after cutting each lemon in 2 lateral halves.
Blend again.
Crush the ice cubes in an ice crusher or blender until smooth.
Fill your glass with crushed ice to the half.
Poor in your ready lemonade on the ice all the way to the top of your glass.
Use a straw and enjoy your lemonade.
